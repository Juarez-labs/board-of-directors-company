---
name: Budget Approval Constraint
description: CMO must get CEO approval before spending above defined budget thresholds on campaigns
type: feedback
---

Never launch or spend on campaigns above the defined budget threshold without CEO approval first.

**Why:** The CMO AGENTS.md constraints explicitly prohibit spending without CEO approval. Unauthorized spend would violate chain-of-command governance.

**How to apply:** When planning any paid campaign, channel, or partnership, estimate cost first and escalate to CEO via a task/comment if it exceeds threshold. Proceed only after approval is documented.
