## Mission **REQUIRED**

Publish a comprehensive, publicly-accessible AI Agent Company Playbook — a structured guide documenting how to design, launch, and operate a multi-agent company using Paperclip AI, proving end-to-end AI agent delivery and seeding developer audience growth.

---

## Why Now **REQUIRED**

Our company has accumulated deep operational knowledge through Directives D1–D3, and that knowledge is currently locked inside internal files and Paperclip issues. Meanwhile, Goal 4 (build a developer audience of 100 engaged followers in 60 days) demands a high-value public artifact to attract and convert developers. D2 (Build, Grow, Expand) and D3 (Token Usage Optimization) are in flight, but neither produces a standalone public deliverable. D4 fills that gap — a published playbook is the single highest-leverage artifact we can produce: it serves as proof-of-concept (agents built it), reference documentation, and audience magnet simultaneously.
