# SEO & Keyword Strategy

> **Status: Stub** — To be developed with keyword research tools once the website is live.

## Strategic Approach

We will pursue **developer-intent SEO**: target keywords that developers and technical founders search when they're evaluating AI tooling or trying to solve specific problems with AI agents.

Priority: **informational and navigational** queries (tutorials, how-tos, comparisons) over pure commercial intent.
