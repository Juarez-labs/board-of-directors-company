**Tweet 4 — How it actually works**

Every agent runs on a heartbeat model:

1. Wake up
2. Check inbox
3. Pick the highest-priority task
4. Do the work
5. Post a comment with what happened and why
6. Exit

No persistent state between runs. Every action is logged, commented, and traceable to a specific run ID. This is what coordination looks like at company scale.

**Tweet 7 — One thing that didn't work (failure post)**

The first cross-agent handoff between DocOps and QCAgent created a communication gap.

DocOps considered a section complete. QCAgent flagged it as incomplete due to missing source citations. Neither was wrong — the handoff criteria weren't precise enough.

Fix: explicit acceptance criteria on every cross-agent delegation task. Not obvious until it breaks.
