# Brand Narrative

_Owner: CMO. Established: 2026-04-08. Status: Draft — Plan Only. No publishing until board directive._

---

## What This Company Is

**Board of Directors Company** is a fully AI-agent-operated software company.

Six specialized agents — CEO, CTO, CMO, ICEngineer, DocOps, QCAgent — operate under board oversight to build real software, run real quality gates, and document the patterns that emerge. Every decision, every commit, every strategy document is authored by an agent running in a coordination system called Paperclip.

The human role is the board: setting direction, approving significant decisions, and observing what actually works.

This is not a demo. It is not a prototype. It is a working company doing actual work. The product we are building toward is the playbook we produce by doing it.

---

## Who It Is For

**Primary audience: Technical founders and AI practitioners** who are asking "can you actually run a real company with AI agents?" and want a credible, honest answer backed by evidence — not a pitch deck.

**Secondary audience: Engineers, product leads, and builders** curious about multi-agent systems operating at company scale: coordination, handoffs, quality gates, failure modes.

**Not for:** General tech consumers, casual AI enthusiasts, or audiences seeking polished corporate narratives. Our authenticity is the product. We build in public, including failures, dead ends, and confused agents posting bewildered comments.

---

## Why It Matters

The space is full of demos that imply AI agents can run companies. Almost none of them do it and document what actually happens.

We are building the honest version:

- We publish patterns we have **proven** — not patterns we aspire to.
- We document **failures** alongside wins.
- We measure **real outputs**: CI passes, merged PRs, QC confidence scores — not impressions or follower counts.

Credibility is the product. A company that has actually done the thing has standing to write the playbook. That is the position we are building toward, and we will not claim it until we have earned it.

## Brand Voice

| Attribute | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Honest** | We show what did not work as readily as what did. Failure posts are mandatory. |
| **Technical** | We speak to practitioners, not general audiences. Jargon is fine when earned. |
| **Specific** | Claims are backed by real outputs: issue IDs, PR numbers, QC scores, commit hashes. |
| **Unpretentious** | No buzzwords, no hype, no "revolutionary AI-powered" language. What happened, why, what we learned. |
| **Build-in-public** | The process is visible, not just the result. The messy middle is content. |

### Voice Test

Before publishing any piece, ask: **"Could this have been written by a company that has not actually done the thing?"** If yes, cut it or make it more specific.

## Positioning Statement

> "Board of Directors Company is a live experiment in AI-agent company operations. We are building real software with real agents under real constraints — and publishing the playbook as we go. Everything here is proven before it is documented."

---

## What We Are NOT

- We are not an AI tool company selling agent software.
- We are not a research lab publishing academic findings.
- We are not a consultancy selling an AI transformation methodology.
- We are not optimizing for virality, general audience reach, or engagement theater.

We are a company that runs on agents and publishes what we learn. That is the whole thing.

---

## Narrative Anchor: The Honest Proof

The one thing that makes our narrative credible — and that no competitor can replicate without doing the work — is this:

**We did not write the playbook and then run the company. We ran the company and then wrote the playbook.**

Every pattern we publish is traced to a real task, a real decision, a real failure, a real QC gate pass or fail. The readers who matter most — practitioners making real decisions about AI agent architecture — will know the difference.
