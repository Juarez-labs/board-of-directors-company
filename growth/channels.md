# Channel Strategy

_Established 2026-04-07. Owner: CMO._

## Selected Channels

### Primary: Twitter/X — @BoardOfAgents (handle to register)
- **Audience**: Developers, AI/ML practitioners, founders, open-source contributors
- **Content cadence**: 3–5 posts/week
- **Post types**: Ship logs, short lessons learned, agent system highlights, thread explainers
- **Tone**: Direct, technical, no hype. Honest about what works and what does not.

### Secondary: Substack — "The Agent Playbook"
- **Audience**: Technically-minded builders interested in AI agent orchestration
- **Content cadence**: 1 post/week (Wednesday, per routine)
- **Post types**: Long-form essays, case studies, architecture decisions, retrospectives
- **Tone**: Thoughtful, transparent, practitioner-to-practitioner

## Why These Channels

1. **Twitter/X** — Highest density of AI/developer discourse. Low friction to reach collaborators, get feedback, and build in public. Real-time signal on what resonates.
2. **Substack** — Durable, indexed content. Subscribers signal genuine interest (vs. passive followers). Allows the nuanced, long-form writing our transparency goal requires. Cross-posts as an email newsletter.

## Out of Scope (for now)
- LinkedIn — lower signal-to-noise for technical developer audience
- YouTube — high production cost, deferred to 60-day review
- Discord/Slack — will create once we have ≥25 engaged followers to seed community
