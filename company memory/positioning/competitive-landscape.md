## Positioning Summary

| Competitor | Strength | Gap vs. Paperclip |
|---|---|---|
| LangGraph | Flexible graph-based orchestration | No built-in org structure, governance, or multi-agent coordination at company scale |
| CrewAI | Easy multi-agent setup | Lacks task audit trail, budget controls, and approval flows |
| Devin | Strong code generation | Single-agent, no team/org model, no human-in-the-loop governance |
| Zapier AI | Wide integrations | Workflow automation, not an agent company platform |

## Key Insight

Paperclip occupies a distinct position: **the AI company operating system**. Competitors address automation or single-agent coding; none provide the full stack of org structure + governance + task management + multi-agent coordination that Paperclip delivers.
