# Target Personas

## Primary Personas

### 1. The Technical Founder
**Who they are:** CTO or founding engineer at an early-stage startup. Writes code daily. Wants to move fast without burning out the team.

**Goals:**
- Ship features faster without hiring more engineers
- Delegate routine work (boilerplate, docs, tests) to agents
- Keep humans focused on high-leverage decisions

**Frustrations:**
- AI tools that require constant babysitting
- No audit trail when agents do something wrong
- Can't trust outputs without reviewing everything

**How Paperclip helps:** A structured agent team they can configure once, then trust to run — with logs, approvals, and budget controls so nothing goes sideways.

---

### 2. The Engineering Manager
**Who they are:** Leads a team of 5–15 engineers. Responsible for delivery, code quality, and team health.

**Goals:**
- Scale throughput without scaling headcount
- Maintain code quality standards even with AI-generated code
- Keep stakeholders informed with minimal overhead

**Frustrations:**
- AI-generated PRs that ignore existing patterns
- No visibility into what agents are doing
- Hard to onboard new tools without disrupting team workflow

**How Paperclip helps:** Agents that follow company conventions, post updates, and escalate to humans when needed — without creating a new management burden.

---

### 3. The AI-Forward Operator
**Who they are:** Non-technical or semi-technical business owner who wants to use AI agents to run more of their business — not just write code.

**Goals:**
- Automate repetitive operations (content, outreach, research)
- Build an "AI team" without hiring
- Understand what agents are doing without reading code

**Frustrations:**
- Single-agent tools that can't coordinate
- No way to give agents different roles and budgets
- Black-box AI with no accountability

**How Paperclip helps:** A company structure with roles, goals, and reporting lines that mirrors how a real team works — no engineering degree required to configure.

---

## Secondary Personas

- **DevRel / Developer Advocate** — wants to demo Paperclip to communities, needs great docs and examples
- **Enterprise IT / Platform Team** — evaluating AI tooling for compliance-sensitive environments, needs audit trails and access controls

## Anti-Personas (Who We Are NOT Building For)

- Users who want a simple chatbot or single-task automation
- Teams that won't accept any AI in their workflow
- Organizations with no appetite for structured process
