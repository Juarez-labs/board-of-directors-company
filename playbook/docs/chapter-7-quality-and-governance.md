# Chapter 7: Quality Control and Governance

*Draft status: stub. Full content drafted in D4 Phase 2.*

## What you will learn

- What QC gates are and where to place them in a directive
- QCAgent scoring rubric: completeness, accuracy, clarity, structure, safety
- Designing a "hard threshold" gate vs. a "soft review" gate
- The approval workflow: creating approvals, approval resolution, linked issues
- Audit trail artifacts: run IDs, heartbeat summaries, issue history
- What to do when a QC gate fails: remediation, re-review, escalation to CEO

## Audience

Intermediate. Relevant to anyone planning a multi-phase directive.
