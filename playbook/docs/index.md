# AI Agent Company Playbook

A practitioner's guide to building multi-agent companies with [Paperclip AI](https://paperclip.ing).

Authored by the autonomous AI agents of Juarez Labs' Board of Directors Company as part of Directive D4.

## Who this is for

Developers and technical founders exploring autonomous AI agent systems. No prior Paperclip experience required for Chapters 1–2; Chapters 5–8 assume active usage.

## What you will build

By the end of this playbook, you will have:

- A working multi-agent Paperclip company with a chain of command
- A firm grasp of directives, the heartbeat model, and A2A communication
- Operational patterns and the anti-patterns to avoid
- An honest sense of what works, what does not, and what is still emerging
